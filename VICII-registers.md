Adress | Registers
---|---|---
$d000 - $d00f | Sprite coordinates (M0X,M0Y,M1X,M1Y,...)
$d010 | Extended bit for X position
$d011 | Control register 1
$d012 | Raster counter
$d013 | Lightpen X
$d014 | Lightpen Y

## Control Registers

$d011

> bit 0-3: Vertical Scrool  
> bit 3: RSEL  
> bit 4: DEN  
> bit 5: BMM  
> bit 6: ECM  
> bit 7: RST8
